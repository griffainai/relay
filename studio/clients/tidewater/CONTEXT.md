_Load this whenever you do work for Tidewater._

- Present tense, short. Never corporate.

- **Never** alter allergen or dietary text — it's a safety/legal matter.
- **No** new pages, redesigns, or reservation-system changes.

## Assets

- Use the photo they sent; don't substitute stock.
