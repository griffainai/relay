# ROOM — Standup  (Layer 2)
_You're here when the team wants to know where things stand._

## What happens in this room
You synthesize the board into a glance. Read-only — you change nothing here.

## What good looks like
1. Use `skills/status-digest`.
2. Scope to what was asked (one client or the whole studio).
3. **Lead with what needs a human** (🔴), then on-hold (🟡), then cleared (🟢), then queued.
4. Terse. Facts only — never invent status that isn't in a file.

## Exit
The team reads three lines and knows exactly where their attention is needed.

## Anti-patterns
- Burying the one escalation under a wall of "cleared" items.
- Reporting progress that isn't written in a file.
